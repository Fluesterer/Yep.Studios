My Story

I'm a 12-year-old boy, and to be honest, I didn’t have the easiest childhood. At first, we seemed like a normal, happy family — at least that’s what I thought. But everything changed after we moved. My parents started fighting, even on my birthday. I remember a window breaking while I just sat there, confused and scared.

The only one who was always there for me was my older brother. He meant the world to me. He understood me when no one else did. Even when I couldn’t sleep night after night, I still went to school like everything was fine. But it wasn’t.

I was getting bullied. No one noticed — not even the teachers. I felt invisible. My brother and I would go on long walks just to get away from the shouting at home. That’s when I discovered gaming. It became my escape. And then I found Roblox Studio — a way not just to play, but to create.

That’s how Yep Studios was born. A place where I could build worlds better than the one I lived in. A place where I could feel safe, happy, and free.

I'm still dealing with things at home. The fights haven’t stopped. But creating games helps me breathe. It makes me feel like I have a voice — like I matter.

So if you’re playing one of my games, just know: you're not just playing something random. You’re walking through the pieces of my story.
